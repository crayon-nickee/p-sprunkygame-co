Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 19, 2025

Sprunky Game ("we," "us," or "our") operates this website providing free online access to play Sprunky. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information from our users. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices and your rights.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect any personal information from our users. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Account information or user profiles
  • Personal preferences or settings
  • Location data or IP addresses
  • Device identifiers or tracking cookies
  • Browsing history or behavioral data

Local Browser Storage Only

  • Browser Local Storage: The Sprunky game uses only your browser's local storage to save game progress, settings, and preferences. This data remains entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.
  • No Server Communication: The game operates entirely in your browser without sending any data to external servers during gameplay.

How We Operate Without Data Collection

Since we do not collect any personal information, our service operates as follows:

  • The Sprunky game runs entirely in your browser using client-side technology
  • All game progress and settings are stored locally on your device
  • No user data is transmitted to our servers during gameplay
  • We provide the game files and website content without tracking users
  • No analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking services are used
  • We maintain the website infrastructure without collecting user information

Third-Party Services

We minimize the use of third-party services to protect your privacy:

  • Content Delivery Networks (CDN): We may use CDNs only to serve game files, images, and static content efficiently. We choose CDN providers that respect user privacy and do not track users.
  • No Analytics Services: We do not use web analytics tools, tracking scripts, or user behavior monitoring services.
  • No Advertising Networks: We do not display advertisements or use ad networks that track users.
  • No Social Media Integration: We do not integrate social media tracking pixels or sharing buttons that collect user data.

Our commitment is to provide the Sprunky game experience without compromising your privacy.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We cannot share what we do not collect. Since we do not collect any personal information:

  • No Data to Share: We have no personal information to share with third parties, sell, or transfer.
  • No Service Provider Access: Our hosting and CDN providers only serve static content and do not have access to user data.
  • Legal Compliance: Since we collect no personal information, there is no user data to disclose even if legally required.
  • Business Transfers: In any business transfer, there would be no user personal information to transfer.

Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no user data for us to retain on our servers. All game data is stored locally in your browser and remains under your complete control. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings. We do not maintain any logs or records of user activity.

Your Rights and Choices

Since we do not collect personal information, you have complete control over your data:

  • Complete Control: All game data is stored locally in your browser under your complete control.
  • Access Data: You can access your locally stored game data through your browser's developer tools (F12 ‚Üí Application/Storage).
  • Clear Data: You can clear all game data by clearing your browser's site data for our domain.
  • No Tracking: We do not use cookies, tracking scripts, or any data collection mechanisms.
  • Privacy by Design: Our service is designed to respect your privacy from the ground up.

For residents of jurisdictions with data protection laws (EU/EEA, California, etc.), you can be assured that we have no personal data to access, correct, delete, or port, as we simply do not collect it.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our service is safe for children of all ages because we do not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age. We are fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) by design.

The Sprunky game operates entirely in the browser without requiring any personal information, account registration, or data submission. Parents can be confident that their children's privacy is protected when using our service.

Parents and guardians: You can verify our privacy practices by reviewing this policy and observing that the game requires no personal information to play.

International Data Transfers

Our servers and third-party service providers may be located in various countries around the world. By using our service, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your country of residence, which may have different data protection laws. We take appropriate measures to ensure your data is protected in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

About Sprunky Game

Sprunky is an original rhythm and music creation game inspired by Incredibox. It features unique characters, sounds, and gameplay mechanics that are original creations. The game allows players to create custom music tracks by combining sound loops and characters in an intuitive drag-and-drop interface.

All game assets, including characters, music, artwork, and animations, are either original works or used under appropriate licenses. We respect intellectual property rights and strive to ensure all content is properly licensed.
